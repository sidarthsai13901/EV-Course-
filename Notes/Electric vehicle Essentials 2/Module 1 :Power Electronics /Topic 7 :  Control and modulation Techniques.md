## Motor Control Unit
The three phases are driven by three half bridges , for a total of  6 power  transistors , These are normally activated by three PWM signals , whose timing and sequence are controlled by an MCU or an integrated driver IC . Three Hall sensors provide position feedback to the microcontroller
